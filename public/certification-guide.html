1. Overview & Why This Matters for GoLive

Microsoft replaced its old Gold/Silver competency model with Solutions Partner designations. The relevant one for GoLive is Solutions Partner for Modern Work — awarded to partners who demonstrate proven skills in Microsoft 365 deployment, security hardening, and growing customer usage.

What GoLive gains upon achieving this designation:
  • Public listing in the Microsoft Marketplace partner directory for Nigeria (marketplace.microsoft.com)
  • Official Solutions Partner badge for website, proposals, email signatures and LinkedIn
  • Up to $6,000/year Azure credits for internal use and demos
  • Up to 25 free Microsoft 365 Business Premium licences for internal team
  • Co-sell eligibility — GoLive deals can be referred by Microsoft's own sales team
  • Access to higher CSP incentive margins and Microsoft partner rebates
⚠️ Note on Marketplace Consulting Listings:

Publishing a Consulting Service listing on Microsoft Marketplace currently requires a US, UK or Canadian seller ID. GoLive's Nigeria registration blocks this route directly. Achieving the Solutions Partner designation gives GoLive an automatic profile in the partner directory and positions GoLive for future listing via distributor arrangement.

4. AB-900 — Start Here (Week 1)

This is GoLive's first and fastest certification to earn. Microsoft launched AB-900 in February 2026 as the replacement for MS-900, with a focus on managing Microsoft 365 Copilot, AI agents, and security administration.

Exam Details

AB-900 Fundamentals

No prerequisites. Open to anyone. Conceptual knowledge — no hands-on lab required. Passing score: 700 / 1000. Duration: 45 minutes.

Cost: $99 USD
Exam Topics

What it covers

Microsoft 365 Copilot fundamentals · AI agent administration · M365 security & compliance · Pricing and licensing concepts · Cloud fundamentals

Study time: 1–2 weeks

How to Register & Study

  • Free study path: learn.microsoft.com → search "Microsoft 365 Copilot and Agent Administration Fundamentals"
  • Free practice assessment: available on the AB-900 certification page at learn.microsoft.com (same team that writes the real exam)
  • Register exam: pearsonvue.com/microsoft → search "AB-900" → book online proctored (no travel required)
  • Equipment needed: Webcam, microphone, quiet room, stable internet
  • Retake policy: Retake after 24 hours if failed. Full fee applies each attempt.
  • Discount tip: Check Partner Center → Learning for exam vouchers (Microsoft sometimes provides partner vouchers)
Note: MS-900 is still valid

If any GoLive team member already holds MS-900, it counts immediately as the intermediate certification — no need to re-sit AB-900. Check partner.microsoft.com → your profile to confirm certifications linked.

5. MS-102 — Microsoft 365 Administrator Expert (Advanced)

This is the key advanced certification for the Modern Work designation. It covers real-world Microsoft 365 tenant administration — exactly what GoLive's technical team does daily for clients.

Exam Details

MS-102 Admin Expert

No formal prerequisites but hands-on experience required. Scenario-based questions. Passing score: 700 / 1000. Duration: ~120 minutes. Renews annually via free online assessment.

Cost: $165 USD
Exam Domains 2026

What it covers

Administer Microsoft 365 (40–45%) · Manage users & groups (35–40%) · Manage licences & service health (10–15%) · Plan hybrid & cloud deployments (5–10%)

Study time: 6–12 weeks

How to Register & Study

  • Free learning path: learn.microsoft.com → search "Microsoft 365 Administrator" — multi-module path covering all exam domains
  • Free sandbox tenant: developer.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/dev-program — 90-day Microsoft 365 E5 developer tenant (free, renewable). Essential for hands-on practice.
  • Hands-on practice areas: User creation, MFA configuration, conditional access policies, SPF/DKIM/DMARC, Exchange Online, SharePoint, Teams admin centre
  • Free practice assessment: available on the MS-102 certification page at learn.microsoft.com
  • Register exam: pearsonvue.com/microsoft → search "MS-102" → online proctored or nearest test centre
  • Recommended study schedule: 1–2 hours per day for 8 weeks = ready to pass
Practical advantage for GoLive:

GoLive's technical team is already configuring M365 tenants, MFA, SPF/DKIM/DMARC and Exchange Online for clients daily. This hands-on experience is exactly what MS-102 tests. The exam is essentially GoLive's day-to-day work — the main gap is learning the exam format and specific Microsoft admin terminology.
